## What is Cangrejob?

**Cangrejob** helps people find work by **reaching out to companies directly**, instead of
getting lost among thousands of applications on the usual job boards.

You upload your résumé, we tell you **which areas fit you best** and **which companies** are
worth writing to, and we prepare a **personalized application** for each one. You review and
decide. We take care of the tedious part.

> Most openings are never posted. Cangrejob helps you knock on those doors.

---

## How it works

| | |
|---|---|
| **1. Upload your CV** | We analyze it and suggest the roles and sectors where you fit best. |
| **2. We find companies for you** | We show you real businesses that may be interested in your profile. |
| **3. We send your application** | A personalized message, with your CV, to each company — from your own email account. |

Fully guided, step by step. Start for free.
